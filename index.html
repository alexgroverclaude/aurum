What It Detects

Every confluence,
automatically mapped.

01 · Sweep

Liquidity
Sweeps

Wicks that push beyond swing highs or lows and close back inside range — a classic sign that smart money has hunted stops before reversing.

Liq. Sweep
02 · Structure

Break of
Structure

Bullish BOS when price closes above the last swing high. Bearish BOS below the last swing low. Marks the confirmed shift in market intent.

BOS
03 · Supply / Demand

Order
Blocks

The last opposing candle before a BOS. Highlighted on-chart and tracked as price returns to retest — one of the highest-probability entry zones in ICT.

OB
04 · Imbalance

Fair Value
Gaps

Three-candle imbalance zones where price moved too fast to fill. Drawn as boxes in real time. Also detects Inverse FVGs when price re-enters a prior gap.

FVG / IFVG
05 · Sessions

Kill
Zones

London, New York, and Asian session timing overlays. Setups formed during kill zones carry significantly more weight in the confluence score.

KZ
06 · Context

Equilibrium
& Daily Bias

50% retracement of the last swing range marks equilibrium. Daily bias set by EMA trend direction — so every alert knows the direction of the bigger picture.

EQ / Bias
How It Works
01

Add to your
TradingView chart

Paste the Pine Script into TradingView's editor and add it to any chart. Works on any market — forex, indices, crypto, stocks — and any timeframe.

02

It scans
in real time

Aurum continuously monitors for all 8 confluence types. Each setup is scored 0–5+ based on how many align simultaneously on the same candle.

03

Get alerted
when setups form

When 3 or more confluences align, TradingView fires an alert. You decide whether to trade — Aurum makes sure you never miss a setup again.
