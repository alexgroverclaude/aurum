Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Who we are

Aurum is a software product providing TradingView Pine Script indicators and trading automation tools. References to "we," "us," or "our" refer to the operator of this website and the Aurum product.

What data we collect

We collect only the data you voluntarily provide:

  • Your email address, when you submit the early access / waitlist form
  • Any correspondence you send via the contact email address

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics scripts. We do not collect IP addresses, device data, or behavioural data.

How we use your data

Your email address is used solely to:

  • Notify you when early access becomes available
  • Send product updates and launch communications you signed up for

We will never sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data storage

Email addresses submitted via the waitlist form are stored in Supabase, a cloud database provider. Data is stored on servers within the EU. Supabase's privacy policy is available at supabase.com/privacy.

Your rights (UK GDPR)

If you are based in the UK or EU, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data at any time
  • Withdraw consent for communications

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@aurum.trade and we will respond within 30 days.

Data retention

We retain waitlist email addresses until Aurum launches publicly, after which you will have the option to convert to a paying customer or be removed from the list. You may request deletion at any time before then.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product develops. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be communicated to waitlist subscribers by email.

Contact
